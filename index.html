What is PostgreSQL?

PostgreSQL is a powerful, open-source object-relational database system with more than 30 years of active development. Known for its stability, extensibility, and advanced feature set, PostgreSQL supports both SQL (relational) and JSON (non-relational) queries. It is widely used across industries to support mission-critical applications due to its ability to handle complex data operations, strong security controls, and compatibility with modern software development practices.

Key Benefits of PostgreSQL

  • - Open Source, Cost-Effective: No licensing fees and community-backed development.
  • - Advanced Performance: Excellent for large data volumes and complex workloads.
  • - Extensible architecture and custom data types
  • - Advanced support for JSON, XML, and full-text search
  • - Active and supportive global community
  • - Cross-platform and cloud-ready
